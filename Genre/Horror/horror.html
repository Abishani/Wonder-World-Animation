Resident Evil

Based on a story of Mutated Monster

Monster House

Monster House is a 2006 American computer-animated supernatural film directed by Gil Kenan in his directorial debut from a screenplay by Dan Harmon, Rob Schrab and Pamela Pettler, about a neighborhood being terrorized by a sentient haunted house during Halloween.

Scary Godmother

Based on the Scary godmother books written Jill Thompson
Directed by Ezekiel Norton
Released on 31st October 2003

Addams Family

Addams Family redefines what it means to be a good neighbor.
Written by Matt Lieberman, Pamela Pettler and Directed by Conrad Vernon, Greg Tiernan.
Released on 11th October 2019

Frankenweenie

Based on a story of a boy's beloved dog which passes away suddenly and he attempts to bring the animal back to life through a powerful science experiment.
Written by Leonard Ripps, Tim Burton
Directed by Tim Burton
Released on 20th September 2012

Paranorman

Based on story of a misunderstood boy takes on ghosts, zombies and grown-ups to save his town from a centuries-old curse.
Written by Chris Butler
Directed by Chris Butler, Sam Fell

Coraline

Based on a story of a adventurous girl named Coraline finding an idealized parallel world behind a secret door in her new home, unaware that the alternative world contains a dark and sinister secret.
Written by Neil Gaiman
Directed by Henry Selick
Released on 5th February 2009.

Corpse Bride

When a shy groom practices his wedding vows in the inadvertent presence of a deceased young woman, she rises from the grave assuming he has married her.
Written by Tim Burton
Directed byTim Burton, Mike Jhonson
Released on 7th September 2005
